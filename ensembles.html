CONCERT BAND

The two ensembles together are taught all year long in multiple sections. Students learn, develop and master various basics and fundamentals on their instruments. They also analyze and perform pieces of repertoire together as a combined ensemble. Outside of class, the entire group rehearses after school two to three times a week.

CONCERT CHOIR

This section of vocalists works through various exercises to become an elite singer. Students work on range, expression and balance as an ensemble. The choir also works on various pieces of repertoire.

POP MUSIC

This class exposes students to an array of musical genres. Music history, theory, analysis and expression are just a few concepts of many that students will cover throughout this semester course.

MARCHING BAND

The pride of our school is displayed in the numerous performances of the Marching Mustangs! The marching band meets for several days of band camp in the last weeks of August to prepare for the Fall season of shows. Performances include all football games and various festivals.

JAZZ BAND

This is an elite ensemble of musicians who perform various styles of jazz such as big band, swing, bebop, fusion and more. This group is a select group and meets after school from winter through spring.

PIT ORCHESTRA

This is a select group of musicians who perform for the spring musical. This is a group that meets after school for numerous rehearsals to prepare for live shows performed in the spring. Musicianship skills are developed further in this group due to the intensity of Broadway musical repertoire!

CHAMBER ENSEMBLES

Marlboro HS is gifted with many talented musicians. Various select groups meet in school and outside of school to rehearse challenging small ensemble repertoire. They have numerous opportunities to perform for the school and the community in addition to concerts.
